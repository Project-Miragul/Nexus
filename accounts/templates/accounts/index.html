What Makes {{ site_name }} Different?

Classic EverQuest with modern conveniences

Free Trade Rules

Firiona Vie-style free trade means almost all items are tradeable. Build your character the way you want with unprecedented flexibility.

Legacy Items Preserved

Legacy item drops remain in game permanently at adjusted rarity. Your favorite items never get nerfed or removed.

Raids Tuned for Smaller Teams

Raid targets are scaled for smaller groups. Open raids are scheduled throughout each week via Discord.

Advanced Web Tools

Character viewer, account-wide inventory search, self-service transfers, DKP tracking, and a full database — all in your browser.

Optional Client Mods

Zeal is permitted — a quality-of-life enhancement for the classic 2002-era TAKP client used by EQEmu players.

Character Transfers

New players can transfer up to three characters from other servers, so you don't start from scratch.

Group Experience Bonus

Extra XP for the 4th, 5th, and 6th group members actively encourages full groups over solo play.

Keyrings Enabled

Available from launch — even Plane of Sky keys go straight to your keyring. No more juggling key inventory.

Server-Wide Channels

/ooc and /auction reach every zone, keeping the community connected no matter where your adventures take you.
